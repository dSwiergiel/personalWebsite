About Me

Making things is my passion. Whether I am at my place of employment working with other developers, at school working on an assignment, or at home working on a personal project, I find that really like the process of creating and tinkering. You've heard the cliche, you know, the one where from an early age they have been interested in knowing how things work, even taking things apart to find out. Yeah, that was me. I'm quite glad too, as many of the skills I have as a developer are self-taught and come from this natural interest to create.

I am the first in my entire family to ever attend college and I am currently attending The University at Albany working towards a combined Computer Science and Applied Mathematics Bachelors degree. This has been both a challanging yet rewarding experience and I am lucky to have such a great group of friends who have helped me along the way.

When I'm not developing software I enjoy working with my 3D that I built a little while back. I've used it to print many parts for around the house as well as custom parts for some of my personal electronic projects.

When I'm not creating I'm likely hanging with friends, enjoying outdoor activities, or sleeping!
